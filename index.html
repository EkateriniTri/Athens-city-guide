Why Athens?

Athens,the city with history over 3000 years

The city took its name after Athena, the Goddess of Wisdom and daughter of Zeus.One of the most ancient cities in the world, Athens Greece is famous as the birthplace of democracy. With a history of over 3,000 years, it is the best town for sightseeing.

It's renowned for its rich history and archaeological ruins, but there's so much more to this amazing city. With pristine beaches, stunning landscapes, a vibrant nightlife, and mouth-watering cuisine, there's something for everyone, making it the ideal place for a getaway.
